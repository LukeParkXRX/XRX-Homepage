ABOUT XRX — eXtended Reality eXperience

The meaning
behind the name

XRX = eXtended Reality eXperience

XRX stands for 'eXtended Reality eXperience,' a name that deeply encapsulates our core vision and identity.

Moving beyond conventional, static visual experiences, we fuse cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence (AI) and eXtended Reality (XR) technologies. Our goal is to evolve digital content into a 'Living Being' that authentically interacts and connects with users.

To make this a reality, XRX has developed a proprietary platform for building Ontologies, designed to foster genuine empathy and profound relationships between AI and humans. Through this platform, our AI does not merely respond to commands; it accurately recognizes individuals, deeply engages with their emotions, and maintains a continuous, evolving relationship.

We believe that only when AI and real people form these lasting, meaningful connections can a true 'eXtended Reality eXperience' be achieved.

Guided by our philosophy of "Breathing Life into AI," XRX seamlessly integrates Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and Mixed Reality (MR) to deliver an entirely new dimension of innovative and immersive 'eXperiences.'
