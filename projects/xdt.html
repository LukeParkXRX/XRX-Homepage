Overview

XDT (eXtended Digital Twin) unifies heterogeneous industrial communication standards — ROS 2, MQTT, OPC-UA, Modbus — into a single real-time 3D visualization layer. Every connected device, sensor, robot, and controller is rendered as a living twin of its physical counterpart.

Field devices are registered via JSON configuration with no custom PLC coding required. The platform layers AI-driven anomaly detection across the entire asset network, triggering predictive maintenance and operational decisions in real time.

What we did

  • Protocol-agnostic device registration (ROS 2 / MQTT / OPC-UA / Modbus)
  • JSON-declarative device onboarding — no PLC coding
  • Real-time 3D digital twin rendering with sub-second sync
  • AI anomaly detection and predictive maintenance across all connected assets

Outcome

XDT is deployed across industrial manufacturing sites and smart facilities, available at xdt.co.kr. It redefines SCADA-era operational visibility for an XR-and-AI-native world.
