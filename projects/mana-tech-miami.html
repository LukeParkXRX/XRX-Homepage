Overview

Through a strategic partnership with Mana Tech, XRX launched its first large-scale North American deployment of AI Virtual Human Kiosks in Miami. Each kiosk is powered by ALIVE Engine, enabling the virtual human to recognize returning visitors, sustain contextual conversations, and adapt over time.

This deployment validates ALIVE Engine's production-readiness outside of controlled environments, demonstrating robust performance in high-traffic public settings.

What we did

  • Partnership agreement with Mana Tech signed for US rollout
  • Deployed AI Virtual Human Kiosks at multiple Miami locations
  • Integrated ALIVE persistent memory for returning-visitor recognition
  • Localized interaction patterns for US English and multi-cultural context

Outcome

The Miami deployment marks XRX's first major presence in the North American market and opens a replicable model for expansion into additional US cities in 2026.
