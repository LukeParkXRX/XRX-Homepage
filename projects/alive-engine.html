Bringing AI into three-dimensional context

ALIVE Engine is XRX's proprietary generative neural framework. It injects real-time behavioral intelligence into immersive 3D environments, transforming static characters and environments into entities that perceive, remember, and respond.

Unlike text-only LLM integrations, ALIVE treats space, identity, and continuity as first-class primitives. Characters running on ALIVE do not reset between interactions — they maintain persistent memory, evolve their understanding of individuals over time, and behave coherently across sessions.

Core capabilities

  • Real-time AI persona with persistent long-term memory
  • Emotional state modeling and relationship graph
  • Behavioral intelligence grounded in 3D spatial context
  • Sub-12ms response latency in production environments
  • Native Unreal Engine and Unity SDK integration
  • Multi-agent orchestration for populated environments
  • Continuous behavioral learning from live interaction data

Where it lives

ALIVE Engine powers ALIVE Park — XRX's AI-powered interactive theme park platform — as well as multiple client deployments including the Samsung EHS AI & XR Safety Education content and Mana Tech's AI Virtual Human Kiosks in Miami.

The engine is also the foundation of XRX's strategic project collaborations with Meta, Toyota (in-car infotainment emotional AI agents), Netflix (interactive media content), and The Walt Disney Company (next-generation interactive IP content).

The methodology

Every ALIVE deployment follows a 4-phase pipeline: Cognitive Mapping → Spatial Architecture → Neural Synthesis → Live Deployment. Each phase is engineered for real-time performance and continuous learning. See the Methodology section for details.
